About This Project

Understanding our employee attrition prediction system

This advanced analytics platform helps organizations predict employee turnover and identify at-risk employees using machine learning algorithms. By analyzing various factors such as job role, income, tenure, and work-life balance, our system provides actionable insights to reduce attrition rates and improve employee retention.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with predictive insights that foster better employee experiences and reduce turnover costs.
